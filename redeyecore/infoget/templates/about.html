About


This is an app developed for the company by an industrious IT sysadmin.

It collects data from several sources, aggregates it, and presents it in a few different ways. This helps us manage our day-to-day operations a little better.

It's called 'RedEye' because it was built almost entirely between 9:00 PM and 2:00 AM, fueled by junk food and Irish whiskey. It's also like an 'eye' because, you know, it lets us see stuff that we couldn't before.

Pretty cool, eh? I think it's cool.
